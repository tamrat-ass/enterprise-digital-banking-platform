🎯 USAGE EXAMPLES
================================================================================

CREATING A DEPARTMENT WITH DIVISIONS (Backend Support):
  POST /api/departments
  {
    "name": "Digital Banking",
    "code": "DIG",
    "divisions": [
      {"name": "Digital Banking Agent", "code": "DBA"},
      {"name": "Mobile Banking", "code": "MB"}
    ]
  }

ADDING DIVISION TO EXISTING DEPARTMENT (UI):
  1. Navigate to /departments
  2. Click on department name to expand
  3. Click "Add Division" button
  4. Fill in division details
  5. Click "Add Division"

VIEWING DEPARTMENT HIERARCHY (UI):
  1. Navigate to /departments
  2. Departments display in expandable cards
  3. Click any department to expand and see divisions
  4. Click again to collapse

MANAGING DIVISIONS (UI):
  - Edit: Click edit icon on division row
  - Delete: Click delete icon and confirm
  - Status: All divisions default to "active"
