STEP 1: OPEN GMAIL SECURITY
───────────────────────────

Go to URL in browser:
  https://myaccount.google.com/security

Sign in if asked.


STEP 2: CHECK 2-STEP VERIFICATION
──────────────────────────────────

On the left, look for: "How you sign in to Google"

STEP 3: GO TO APP PASSWORDS
────────────────────────────

Type this URL:
  https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

You should see two dropdown menus.


STEP 4: SELECT MAIL
───────────────────

First dropdown: "Select the app"
  Click it
  Choose: Mail


STEP 5: SELECT DEVICE
─────────────────────

Second dropdown: "Select the device"
  Click it
  Choose: Windows Computer


STEP 6: CLICK GENERATE
──────────────────────

Look for button: "Generate"
  Click it

Wait for result...


STEP 7: COPY PASSWORD
─────────────────────

Google shows yellow box with:

  abcd efgh ijkl mnop

This is your APP PASSWORD!

✅ Copy it exactly (with spaces)


STEP 8: SEND TO ME
